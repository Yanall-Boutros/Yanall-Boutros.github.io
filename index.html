About Yanall

Graduated from University of California, Santa Cruz in 4 years with a Bachelor of Science (B.S.) in Physics and B.S in Computer Science. I spend most of my time teaching myself skills or hobbies such as: making music, learning cybersecurity, earning my pilots license, and DIY raspberry pi projects. Finding pointless ways to integrate applied math and AI techniques into my hobbies is another fun way I kill time, however it usually leaves me desiring more. My goal is to land a research position anywhere which applies artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques, and to support myself with the services advertised on this website.
